Step 1: Create a New AWS Account

Setting up your AWS foundation

Before you begin, you'll need:

  • A unique email address (not previously used with AWS)
  • A valid credit card for verification
  • A phone number for verification
1

Navigate to the AWS Free Tier page and click Create a Free Account.

2

Enter your email address, password, and AWS account name.

3

Provide your contact information and credit card details.

4

Complete the phone verification step.

5

Choose the Basic Support - Free plan.

Important: The user you are now logged in as is the root user, which has unrestricted access to all resources. For security reasons, you should avoid using the root user for everyday tasks.
