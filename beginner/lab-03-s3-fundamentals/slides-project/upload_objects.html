Step 2: Upload and Manage Objects

Working with files in your S3 bucket

Objects are the fundamental entities stored in Amazon S3. An object consists of data, key (name), and metadata.

1

Navigate to your newly created bucket in the S3 console.

2

Click Upload to add files to your bucket.

3

Click Add files and select files from your computer.

4

Optionally, expand Properties to set storage class and metadata.

5

Click Upload to complete the process.

6

After upload, click on an object to view its properties and URL.

AWS CLI Alternative:

# Upload a file to your bucket
aws s3 cp myfile.txt s3://your-name-test-bucket-123/

# List objects in your bucket
aws s3 ls s3://your-name-test-bucket-123/

# Download an object from your bucket
aws s3 cp s3://your-name-test-bucket-123/myfile.txt .
